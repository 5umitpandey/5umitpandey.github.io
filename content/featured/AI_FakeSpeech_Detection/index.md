This project develops a deep learning–based system to detect *AI‑generated speech*, combining **WaveGAN, Wav2Vec2 transformers, & XAI** tools. It augments datasets with advanced audio transformations, trains both *transformer, CNN models*, & evaluates them. Aiming to support audio forensics & combat misuse of **synthetic voices in fraud, misinformation, & media manipulation**.
